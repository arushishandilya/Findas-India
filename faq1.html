Insurance planning is the process of assessing your risks and determining the right coverage to protect yourself and your family from financial hardships caused by unforeseen events.

It involves evaluating life, health, property, and liability insurance needs to ensure complete financial security.

Insurance planning helps you mitigate financial risks from accidents, illnesses, property damage, or untimely death.

It ensures your loved ones remain financially secure even when life throws unexpected challenges your way.

Common types include life insurance, health insurance, home insurance, motor insurance, and personal accident coverage.

Your needs may vary based on your lifestyle, assets, dependents, and financial goals.

The coverage should be enough to replace your income, pay off debts, and cover future expenses like education and retirement for dependents.

A financial advisor can help calculate your ideal coverage amount based on your situation.
