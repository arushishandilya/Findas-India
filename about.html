About Us

Investing and insurance can be complex and overwhelming for many people, and that's where FINDAS comes in. With over two decades of experience in the field, we have a deep understanding of the various financial products and investment options available to our clients. At FINDAS, we believe in transparency and ethical business practices. We value knowledge sharing with our clients, empowering them to become financially literate investors. We want our clients to feel confident and in control of their finances, and we strive to provide them with a holistic view of their financial lives. Our approach to investing involves a judicious blend of multiple assets and financial products, tailored to each client's unique needs and goals. We understand that implementing an investment plan can be daunting, which is why we provide personalized support and guidance throughout the process.

About Samir Machawe

Samir Machawe is a Author, TLC Certified Financial Freedom Coach, Investor, YouTuber, Podcaster, Avid reader, World Traveller and Financially free. He is also a member of BNI Alpha Nashik and TLC Sirius. With a passion for helping people achieve financial independence and live their dream life, Samir Machawe has dedicated his career to sharing his expertise and insights with others.

As a financial freedom coach, Samir works with individuals and businesses to help them develop a comprehensive investment strategies that aligns with their goals and values. His coaching programs cover a wide range of topics, from budgeting and saving to investing and retirement planning. Samir's goal is to help his clients achieve financial freedom and build wealth for themselves and their families.

In addition to his coaching work, Samir Machawe is also a successful author. His book, "25 Habits That Make You Rich," is a comprehensive guide to building wealth and achieving financial independence. In the book, Samir Machawe shares his personal journey to financial freedom and provides readers with 25 practical tips and strategies for achieving their own financial goals.
